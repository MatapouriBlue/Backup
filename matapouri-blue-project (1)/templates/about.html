About Matapouri Blue

A unique holiday experience

Matapouri Blue offers an extraordinary escape that combines luxury accommodation with the pristine beauty of New Zealand's Northland coast. Our property provides an intimate retreat where guests can disconnect from the everyday and reconnect with nature.

Experience the tranquility of our secluded location while enjoying modern amenities and personalized service. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a change of scenery, Matapouri Blue delivers an unforgettable holiday experience.

Accommodation and facilities

Our thoughtfully designed accommodation features contemporary styling with natural materials that complement the surrounding landscape. Each space is carefully curated to provide comfort while maintaining a connection to the outdoor environment.

Modern facilities include well-appointed kitchens, comfortable living areas, and private spaces designed for relaxation. All amenities are provided to ensure your stay is both comfortable and memorable.

Discover Kinloch

The Kinloch area offers a wealth of natural attractions and outdoor activities. From pristine beaches to native bush walks, there's something for every type of traveler.

Explore secluded bays, enjoy water sports, or simply relax on some of New Zealand's most beautiful coastline. The area is rich in both natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Your Hosts

Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your stay at Matapouri Blue exceeds expectations. With extensive local knowledge and a passion for hospitality, we're here to help you make the most of your time in this beautiful region.

We take pride in providing personalized service while respecting your privacy and independence. Whether you need recommendations for activities, dining, or simply want to know the best spots to watch the sunset, we're here to help.
